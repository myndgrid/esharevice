How esharevice keeps every trade honest

Vouched, not anonymous

Every account is vouched for by a neighbour already on the platform. Three back-outs and your listings dim until you reach out and apologise.

The ledger keeps the receipts

Every proposal, counter, and "we're square" is on a thread you can both look back on. No verbal agreements lost to bad memories.

Disputes get a neighbour, not a bot

If a trade goes sideways, a third neighbour you've both traded with can vouch. No support ticket, no scripted response.
