How it works

Three steps. No tokens. No algorithm.

Trades are negotiated by the two people involved. We handle discovery and the ledger. The rest is on you and your neighbour.

Step 01

Find the thing that already exists nearby.

Search is full-text, weighted by what each listing offers and what its provider needs in return.
