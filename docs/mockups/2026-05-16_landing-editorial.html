02 How esharevice keeps every trade honest The ledger, in plain language

No money changes hands — so the only currency is your word.

01

Vouched, not anonymous.

Every account is vouched for by a neighbour already on the platform. Three back-outs and your listings dim until you reach out and apologise.

1,247 active members · 4,816 vouches issued
02

The ledger keeps the receipts.

Every proposal, counter-offer, and "we're square" lives on a thread you can both look back on. No verbal agreements lost to memory.

0 disputes lost to "he said / she said" — that's the whole point
03

A dispute gets a neighbour, not a bot.

If a trade goes sideways, a third neighbour you've both traded with is asked to weigh in. No support ticket, no scripted reply.

Median resolution time: 3 days · 4 hr 12 min
